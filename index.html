Highlights

Organizing committee meeting.

On March 5, the meeting of the organizing committee of the Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association was held under the leadership of Ara Sargsyan, the president of the Association. During the meeting, issues of organization of the "Armenia Open Championship" tournament were discussed, which will be held on April 15.

06.03.2023

Qualification exam at the “Crystal” club.

On the 5th February at the “Crystal” club of Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association, qualification exam was held by the technical committee.

05.02.2023

Qualification exam at the “Sparta” club.

On the 24th December at the “Sparta” club of Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association, qualification exam was held by the technical committee.

25.12.2022

Technical seminar with Grand Master Vasilis Alexandris.

Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association organized for the first time in Armenia technical seminar of black belt 9th dan Vasilis Alexandris, by expanding its cooperation with international masters.

26.11.2022

GTF World Cup Championship.

Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association participated Global Taekwon-Do Federation’s World Cup Championship in georgian capital Tbilisi, taking 15 medals.

Gold 11
Silver 3
Bronze 1

30.10.2022

First international championship of Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association, “Great Armenia”.

Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association held for the first time "Great Armenia" international championship at "Dinamo" sports Non governmental organization's hall, on 1st May. At the championship participated 150 athletes from Armenia and Russian Federation. International Taekwon-Do Federation's Umpire Committee's Member Petros Mikaelyan was present at the championship.

01.05.2022

First pan-Armenian seminar of Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association.

The first Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association's pan-Armenian seminar was held on 5th of December of this year. The seminar was held by President of ANTA, black belt 6th dan Ara Sargsyan, Coaches of ANTA, black belt 3th dan Grigorios-Isaak Demirchyan, Khachik Sargsyan. The seminat had 100 participants.

05.12.2021

Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association’s Cup Championship.

Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association's Cup Championship was held on 16th of October, at "Dinamo" sports Non-governmental organization's hall. At the championship participated 120 athletes.
