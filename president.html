Armenian National Taekwon-Do Association

President

Ara Sargsyan was born in 1981 in Yerevan, in the family of a pedagogue. He studied at school N 120, after which he continued his education at the “Imastaser” college of ASPU. He graduated from the college in 1998. In the same year he continued his education at the Physics and Astronomy Department of the ASPU Faculty of Physics and Mathematics, graduated in 2003. From 2013 to 2014 he studied at the Armenian State Institute of Physical Culture of the Republic of Armenia, receiving a master's degree. He has been practicing Eastern martial arts since 1990. 2003 He was drafted into the Armed Forces of the Republic of Armenia and served in the Army Central Sports Club. In 2000 he became the champion of the CIS countries, in 2002 - the champion of Eurasia, in 2004 - the bronze medalist of the kickboxing world championship. In the same year he won the bronze medal at the Taekwon-Do World Championships. He is the 2010 and 2014 World Champion. He has the 6th degree of international class. In 2003 he founded the Global Taekwon-Do Federation of the Republic of Armenia. In 2016, he founded the National Taekwondo-Do Association of Armenia, which is a member of the International Taekwon-Do Federation (ITF), the All European Taekwondo Federation (AETF).
